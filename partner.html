What we need from you

About Your Property

  • Property name / title

  • Maximum number of guests
  • Number of bedrooms & bathrooms
  • Short description for travelers

Location

  • Full address
  • City & country
  • Distance to nearest padel court
  • Name of nearest padel center (if known)

Amenities

  • Private padel court on property (yes/no)
  • Swimming pool
  • WiFi, air conditioning, parking
  • BBQ / outdoor area
  • Fully equipped kitchen
  • Washing machine

Pricing & Booking

  • Price per night (€)

  • Full name
  • Email address

You pay a flat monthly fee of €19.95. Your property is listed on Lobii and padel travelers worldwide can discover it. You manage all bookings and payments directly with your guests — Lobii does not take any commission on bookings. We simply drive the traffic to you.
